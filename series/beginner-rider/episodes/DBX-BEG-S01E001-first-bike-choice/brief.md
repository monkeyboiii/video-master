# Brief — DBX-BEG-S01E001 first-bike-choice

## Viewer (one concrete person)

Adult beginner, ~30, has a real budget (~$5–6k / ~2万元) saved for a first dirt bike.
Zero moto background. Is browsing marketplace listings tonight, drawn to the biggest,
fastest-looking bike the money buys, and quietly worried about buying wrong — too tall,
too powerful, too expensive — and confirming everyone's warnings that "you'll hurt
yourself on that thing."

## Pain point, in the viewer's words

- en-US: "What dirt bike should I buy as a beginner? I don't want to waste money on
  something I'm scared to ride."
- zh-CN: "新手第一台越野摩托到底怎么选？预算就这么多，怕买大了骑不动，也怕买了就后悔。"

## One-sentence viewer benefit (the core conclusion this video proves)

Pick your first dirt bike by fit and control, not power: a used mellow 4-stroke in the
230–250 trail class that lets you plant your feet — and keep enough budget back for gear —
and you'll actually keep riding.
