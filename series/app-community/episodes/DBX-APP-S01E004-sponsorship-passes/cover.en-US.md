## Title variants (ranked; label each with its opening formula A/B/C/D)

1. "I built ad slots because I hate ads." — formula: **D counterintuitive** — why: it is the
   literal first line and a genuine contradiction; the viewer has to stay to see it resolved.
2. "The only ad I'd let into my app." — formula: D counterintuitive — why: same tension, shorter,
   reads as a promise about taste rather than a confession.
3. "Riders don't click ads. They click riders." — formula: A pain point, aimed at the buyer —
   why: sharpest for the track owners and moto brands we want commenting.
4. "Get seen by real riders — 40 passes, capped and rotated." — formula: C benefit promise —
   why: concrete and scarce, for buyers already looking for reach.

## Cover concept

- Background image intent: founder frame-right (tone-mapped, natural colour), the **Sponsorships
  pass card** pinned in the reserved blank space at frame-**left** — this episode's own visual
  language — and the DirtBikeX **mark** small in the top-right corner, where the logo retires.
- On-cover text (≤2 lines): **"I built ad slots / because I hate ads."**
- Aspect(s) needed per platform: tiktok/reels/ytshorts 9x16.
- Emotional read at a glance: a builder admitting something against his own interest, with the
  receipts on screen.
