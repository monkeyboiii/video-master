# Brief — DBX-APP-S01E001 made-for-riders

Launch promo for the DirtBikeX iOS app. Series opener for APP · en-US first · ~17s ·
TikTok / Reels / Shorts.

**Direction locked (this round):** belonging-led spine, founder mission compressed into
the pivot; three features shown-not-told in one montage (10 languages · paste-a-link
embed · sponsorship-for-all); style is modern/minimal for transitions + subtitles,
animation-heavy only on the feature explainers, SFX pop+whoosh on the embed render.
Beats live in `manifest.yml`; words in `script.en-US.md`; shots in `storyboard.md`.

## Viewer (one concrete person)

A dirt bike rider whose passion gets shrugged off by the people around them — a spouse
sick of hearing about it, coworkers who don't get it, no one local to actually talk
bikes with. They ride or follow the sport but feel a little alone in it.

## Pain point, in the viewer's words

- en-US: "Everyone in my life is tired of hearing about my dirt bike. I've got no one to
  actually share this with."
- zh-CN: deferred — the zh-CN launch is a separate cut with its own hook (see go/no-go).

## One-sentence viewer benefit (the core conclusion this video proves)

There's a whole global community of people who care about dirt bikes as much as you do —
and it's one download away.

## Five-dimension scores (1–5, with one line of justification each)

| Dimension | Score | Why |
|-----------|------:|-----|
| User relevance 用户相关 | 5 | Every enthusiast has felt their obsession dismissed by the people around them. |
| Emotional trigger 情绪触发 | 5 | Sting of being dismissed → vindication ("I do") → belonging. This is the viral engine. |

## Topic formula (one line)

Pain (nobody around me cares about the thing I love) + solution (a global dirt bike
community app) + benefit (find your people — one download away).

## Designed reactions (the judgment test — all four must have real answers)

- Like because: the vindication — "finally, someone said it."
- Save because: weak here by design — a launch teaser is emotional, not a saveable
  artifact. Not a primary goal; don't force a fake checklist to manufacture saves.
- Comment because: we ask directly — "tag the person in your life who thinks you're
  crazy for loving this." This is the reach engine.
- Download because (the launch conversion, in place of follow): join the community —
  link in bio / description. Follow is secondary.
