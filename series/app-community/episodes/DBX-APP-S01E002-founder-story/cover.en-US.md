## Cover concept

- Background image intent: founder to camera (warm window light, helmet in frame) — the real, un-glossy look the story trades on. A tone-mapped frame from `01_hook` or `03_addictive` (natural color, not pale).
- On-cover text (≤2 lines): **"The hardest part isn't the riding."** — high contrast, bottom-third safe.
- Aspect(s) needed per platform: tiktok/reels/ytshorts 9x16; (add rednote 3x4 if we localize there later).
- Emotional read at a glance: honest, a little vulnerable, "this guy is one of us."

## First frame (0.0s)

Founder mid-sentence, slight punch-in already moving, streaming caption beginning to type "**Nobody** tells you this…". No logo, no title card — a face and a hook, so the first second is a person, not a brand.
